About Local Region

This site is especially good for those looking to travel or have traveled and are looking for areas around them which are accessible. You can find your current location and also search for any nearby destination all by looking up the region in which you're staying at. The map is very flexible and shows nearly any given location at any given time which makes it ideal for staying up to date with the newest and latest restaurants or companies newly added to the area.
